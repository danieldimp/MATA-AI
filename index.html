About

MATA AI is the creative identity of Daniel Mata, Creative Director & Art Director with over 12 years in top-tier advertising at agencies such as TBWA, Publicis, VML, Ganem, BBDO, DDB, and many more. With specialization in cinematic, design-driven, and generative productions for visionary brands, the studio combines proven expertise across banking, sports, automotive, technology, consumer goods, beverages, and entertainment.
